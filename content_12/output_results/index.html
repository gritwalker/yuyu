What do you do after class?

A#1
你下课以后做什么？
nǐ xià kè yǐ hòu zuò shén me?
What do you do after class?
B#2
我去超市买东西。
wǒ qù chāo shì mǎi dōng xi.
I go to the supermarket to buy things.
A#3
你想买什么？
nǐ xiǎng mǎi shén me?
What do you want to buy?
B#4
我想买点儿牛奶和鸡蛋。
wǒ xiǎng mǎi diǎnr niú nǎi hé jī dàn.
I want to buy some milk and eggs.
