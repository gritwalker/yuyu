How do you go to the company every day?

A#1
你每天怎么去公司？
nǐ měi tiān zěn me qù gōng sī?
How do you go to the company every day?
B#2
我坐地铁上班。
wǒ zuò dì tiě shàng bān.
I take the subway to work.
A#3
坐地铁方便吗？
zuò dì tiě fāng biàn ma?
Is taking the subway convenient?
B#4
很方便，不用担心堵车。
hěn fāng biàn, bú yòng dān xīn dǔ chē.
It's very convenient, no need to worry about traffic jams.
