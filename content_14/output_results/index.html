It might rain tomorrow.

A#1
明天可能会下雨。
míng tiān kě néng huì xià yǔ.
It might rain tomorrow.
B#2
那我们还去爬山吗？
nà wǒ men hái qù pá shān ma?
Then, are we still going hiking?
A#3
如果下雨，我们就不去了。
rú guǒ xià yǔ, wǒ men jiù bù qù le.
If it rains, we won't go.
B#4
好吧，我们在家休息。
hǎo ba, wǒ men zài jiā xiū xi.
Alright, we'll rest at home.
