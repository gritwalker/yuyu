When are you free?

A#1
你什么时候有空？
nǐ shén me shí hou yǒu kòng?
When are you free?
B#2
我这个周末有空。
wǒ zhè ge zhōu mò yǒu kòng.
I am free this weekend.
A#3
我们一起去看书吧？
wǒ men yì qǐ qù kàn shū ba?
Shall we go read books together?
B#4
好的，图书馆见。
hǎo de, tú shū guǎn jiàn.
Okay, see you at the library.
