How do I get to that restaurant?

A#1
那个饭店怎么走？
nà gè fàn diàn zěn me zǒu?
How do I get to that restaurant?
B#2
往前走，在第二个路口右转。
wǎng qián zǒu, zài dì èr gè lù kǒu yòu zhuǎn.
Go straight ahead and turn right at the second intersection.
A#3
离这儿远吗？
lí zhèr yuǎn ma?
Is it far from here?
B#4
不远，就在超市对面。
bù yuǎn, jiù zài chāo shì duì miàn.
Not far, it's right across from the supermarket.
