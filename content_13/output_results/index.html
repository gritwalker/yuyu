Can you drive?

A#1
你会开车吗？
nǐ huì kāi chē ma?
Can you drive?
B#2
我会，但是开得不快。
wǒ huì, dàn shì kāi de bù kuài.
I can, but I don't drive fast.
A#3
你有车吗？
nǐ yǒu chē ma?
Do you have a car?
B#4
我打算明年买一辆车。
wǒ dǎ suàn míng nián mǎi yí liàng chē.
I plan to buy a car next year.
