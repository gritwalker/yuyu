Can you speak Chinese?

A#1
你会说汉语吗？
nǐ huì shuō hàn yǔ ma ？
Can you speak Chinese?
B#2
我会说一点儿。
wǒ huì shuō yì diǎn ér 。
I can speak a little.
A#3
你学了多长时间了？
nǐ xué le duō zhǎng shí jiān le ？
How long have you been studying?
B#4
我学了一年了。
wǒ xué le yī nián le 。
I have been studying for a year.
