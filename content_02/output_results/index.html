Going to a Coffee Shop

A#1
你想去哪儿？
nǐ xiǎng qù nǎ ér ？
Where do you want to go?
B#2
我想去咖啡店。
wǒ xiǎng qù kā fēi diàn 。
I want to go to a coffee shop.
A#3
你想喝什么？
nǐ xiǎng hē shén me ？
What do you want to drink?
B#4
我想喝美式咖啡。
wǒ xiǎng hē měi shì kā fēi 。
I want to drink American coffee.
