Taking a taxi

A#1
师傅，去机场要多久？
shī fu, qù jī chǎng yào duō jiǔ?
Taking a taxi
B#2
大概需要四十分钟。
dà gài xū yào sì shí fēn zhōng.
It takes about forty minutes.
A#3
我有点赶时间，能快点吗？
wǒ yǒu diǎn gǎn shí jiān, néng kuài diǎn ma?
I'm in a bit of a hurry, can you go faster?
B#4
没问题，我会走近路。
méi wèn tí, wǒ huì zǒu jìn lù.
No problem, I'll take a shortcut.
